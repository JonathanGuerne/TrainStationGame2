About the Game

Imagine this: you have a full day ahead of you with nothing planned (Yes I know, bear with me). This game is the perfect solution for such a day (if it really existed). What will you need? Ideally a daily pass for train Yes it works also with a yearly pass, thanks for reminding me you got one... The rest is quite simple for you, because you have nothing to do expect following the journey planned by the game from station to station.

But how does it works? Glad you asked! The game will look at your current location and find the closest train station. From there it will pick a departing train from the coming hour and a random station for you to disembark. Once you arrive at your destination, the game will repeat the process.

How far away will you get? Will you end up in a city or in the middle of nowhere? Will you end up in the town next door? All bets are off at this point and that is the beauty of the game!
